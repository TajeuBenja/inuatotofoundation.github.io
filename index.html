About Inua Toto Foundation

Inua Toto Foundation was established with a vision to create a society where every child, orphan, and widow has the opportunity to thrive. Our name "Inua Toto" means "Uplift the Child" in Swahili, reflecting our core mission.

We work tirelessly to provide educational support, healthcare, economic empowerment, and social integration for our beneficiaries. Our approach focuses on sustainable solutions that create lasting change in communities.

With dedicated volunteers, partners, and supporters, we continue to expand our reach and deepen our impact across communities.
